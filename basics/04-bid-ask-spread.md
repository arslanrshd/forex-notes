# Bid, Ask & Spread

## Bid

The **Bid** is the highest price a buyer is willing to pay for a currency pair.

## Ask

The **Ask** is the lowest price a seller is willing to accept for a currency pair.

## Spread

The **Spread** is the difference between the **Bid** and **Ask** price.

## Example

```text
Bid: 1.1050
Ask: 1.1052
Spread: 2 pips
```

## Key Points

* **Bid** → Buyer's price
* **Ask** → Seller's price
* **Spread** → Difference between Bid and Ask
* The spread is the cost of entering a trade.

## Summary

Every Forex trade has two prices: **Bid** and **Ask**. The difference between them is called the **Spread**, which represents the transaction cost of the trade.
