# Forex Brokers

## Definition

A **Forex broker** is a company that provides traders with access to the Forex market.

Without a broker, individual traders cannot trade in the global Forex market.

## What Does a Broker Do?

* Connects traders to the Forex market.
* Executes buy and sell orders.
* Provides a trading account.
* Offers trading platforms like **MT4** and **MT5**.

## Key Points

* A broker acts as a **middleman** between traders and the Forex market.
* Brokers make Forex trading possible for retail traders.
* Different brokers may offer different spreads, leverage, and account types.

## Summary

A Forex broker is your gateway to the Forex market. It allows you to place trades and access the market through a trading platform.
