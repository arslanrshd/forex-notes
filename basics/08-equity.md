# Equity

## Definition

**Equity** is the real-time value of your trading account.

It is calculated by adding or subtracting the floating profit or loss from your account balance.

## Formula

```text
Equity = Balance + Floating Profit/Loss
```

## Example

```text
Account Balance: $1,000
Floating Profit: +$50

Equity = $1,050
```

```text
Account Balance: $1,000
Floating Loss: -$30

Equity = $970
```

## Key Points

* Equity changes in real time as the market moves.
* It includes both your account balance and any floating profit or loss.
* When no trades are open, **Equity = Balance**.

## Summary

Equity represents the current value of your trading account after including any unrealized profits or losses from open trades.
