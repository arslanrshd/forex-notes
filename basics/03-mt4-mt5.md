# MT4 & MT5

## Definition

**MT4 (MetaTrader 4)** and **MT5 (MetaTrader 5)** are trading platforms used to trade the Forex market.

They allow traders to view charts, analyze the market, and place buy or sell orders.

## Key Points

* MT4 and MT5 are **trading platforms**, not brokers.
* You need a **Forex broker account** to trade on MT4 or MT5.
* They provide real-time price charts.
* They allow traders to execute and manage trades.

## MT4 vs MT5

* **MT4:** Designed mainly for Forex trading.
* **MT5:** A newer version with more features and support for additional markets.

## Summary

MT4 and MT5 are trading platforms that connect to your broker's account, allowing you to analyze the market and execute trades.
