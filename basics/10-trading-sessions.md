# Forex Trading Sessions

Forex operates **24 hours a day, 5 days a week** through four major trading sessions.

## Trading Sessions (Pakistan Time)

| Session  | Winter             | Summer             |
| -------- | ------------------ | ------------------ |
| Sydney   | 3:00 AM – 12:00 PM | 2:00 AM – 11:00 AM |
| Tokyo    | 5:00 AM – 2:00 PM  | 4:00 AM – 1:00 PM  |
| London   | 1:00 PM – 10:00 PM | 12:00 PM – 9:00 PM |
| New York | 6:00 PM – 3:00 AM  | 5:00 PM – 2:00 AM  |

## Key Points

* Forex trading is available **24/5**.
* The market moves through **Sydney**, **Tokyo**, **London**, and **New York** sessions.
* Trading activity and liquidity vary from one session to another.
* The **London** and **New York** sessions are generally the most active.

## Summary

The Forex market operates continuously by moving through four major trading sessions around the world, allowing traders to participate almost any time during the trading week.
