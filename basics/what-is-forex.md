
# What is Forex?

## Definition

**Forex** (Foreign Exchange) is the global market where one currency is exchanged for another.

It is the **largest financial market in the world**, with a daily trading volume of over **$7 trillion**.

## Key Points

* Forex stands for **Foreign Exchange**.
* It involves exchanging one currency for another.
* It is the largest financial market in the world.
* Daily trading volume exceeds **$7 trillion**.

## Example

Exchanging **Pakistani Rupees (PKR)** for **US Dollars (USD)** is a simple example of Forex.
